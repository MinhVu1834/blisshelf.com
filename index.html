Laser Heist

Spy mode activated! Transform your home into a thrilling laser maze and see who can pull off the ultimate jewel heist.

Build The Bar

Set Up the Laser Maze

Create a web of “lasers” using colored string and tape. Attach the string to walls or furniture at various heights to make the maze challenging and fun to navigate.

Place the Jewels

Place four “jewels” (like small toys or wrapped candies) throughout the maze. Keep them visible, but position them where careful maneuvering is required to reach.

Navigate and Collect

Take turns attempting to collect all the jewels without touching the “lasers.” Use a timer to see who can complete the heist the fastest!

Enhance Your Laser Maze

Take your mission to the next level with glow-in-the-dark string or LED lights to make your maze even more thrilling in the dark!
