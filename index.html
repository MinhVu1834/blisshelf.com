Build The Bar

Pick Your Flavors

Start with classic flavors like vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry—a single container of "Neapolitan" conveniently offers all three. For added variety, consider including a unique option such as mint chocolate chip, cookies and cream, or a fruity sorbet.

Cones and Bowls

Provide a couple of serving options such as waffle cones, sugar cones, or colorful bowls to enhance the experience.

Tasty Toppings

Arrange toppings like sprinkles, chocolate chips, marshmallows, and gummy bears in small bowls or jars for easy access and a visually appealing setup. Add a variety of Sundae syrups like caramel and fudge for the finishing touch.

Feeling Ambitious?

Try making homemade ice cream together! You can use an ice cream maker or a simple no-churn recipe. This is a fun way to teach kids about the science behind making ice cream.
